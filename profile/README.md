### 🚀 About Our Organization

**Nova Co.** is a dedicated collective of developers focused entirely on the Minecraft backend and modding ecosystem. We specialize in engineering robust APIs, crafting custom server plugins, and maintaining highly optimized forks of existing mods to deliver unparalleled performance and stability for specialized servers.

### 🎯 Current Focus & Expertise

We thrive on solving complex technical challenges within Minecraft's infrastructure. Our core development areas strictly include:

- **Custom Mods & Plugins:** Architecting and developing bespoke modifications and server-side plugins from the ground up to introduce tailored gameplay mechanics.
- **Premium Forks & Maintenance:** Forking, refactoring, and maintaining existing mods and plugins. We focus on deep optimization and sophisticated bug resolution (such as resolving camera shake and freeze issues within cinematic systems) to create stable, premium builds.
- **Robust API Development:** Designing clean, scalable, and developer-friendly APIs that allow seamless integration and extensibility across different Minecraft server environments.
